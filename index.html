Frequently Asked Questions

Will lunch be provided ?

Yes. Lunch will be provided to all the participants.

Reporting time ?

Participants should report at 9.00 AM

How many events can a participant participate in ?

A participant can register for one technical event and one non-technical event. If participants in a technical event do not advance to the second round, they will have the opportunity to participate in a non-technical event of their choice.

Is Guardian allowed ?

No, only the participants are allowed.

Will OD recommendation letters be provided ?

No recommendation letter will be provided instead you will receive an invite from our side after registration.
