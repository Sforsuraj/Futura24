CODING EVENT- Code-Verse Crisis

Team Dynamics:

Form a duo, representing your Spider-Verse team.

Event Structure:

Step into the Code-Verse with three thrilling rounds: conquer challenges, crack codes, and claim victory!

Round 1: Collider Code (Online)– Enter the Code-Verse

  • The first round takes place on the HackerRank platform, where either one of the teammates must participate.

    • Round 2: Code Beyond Reality (10:00– 11:00 A.M.)– Crack the Multiverse

  • This round is a hint-based event, much like navigating the mysteries of the multiverse.
  • Each question comes with 3 hints—the earlier you crack the code, the more points you earn.
  • The 1st hint comes with no reduction of marks, but as time passes and more hints are revealed, your score will decrease with each additional hint.

    • Round 3: Leap of Code (11:30– 12:30 P.M.)– The Final Showdown

  • In this round, teams will face the ultimate coding challenge: pseudocode puzzles.
  • You’ll need to analyse the logic behind the given pseudocode and translate it into a fully functioning program.
  • Points will be awarded both for understanding the logic and for writing the code, with bonus points for speed.
  • The faster you code, the higher you’ll climb in the Spider-Verse rankings!

    • Unlock your potential—code with creativity and conquer the multiverse!
